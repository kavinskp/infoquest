Instructions


  • Individual participation.
  • By keeping your pages simple, your website will be easier to use.The Webpages Must be as simple as possible. Functionality completeness gets more credit.
  • The overall look and feel of your site must be in a way easily understandable to users.
  • Pages should be well-organized with a top-down design so that visitors can easily browse through the different sections of your site.
  • When choosing colors, use a consistent palette of colors that don't clash and make sure there is a strong contrast between the text and the background.
  • Make sure to check your website in multiple browsers to make sure everything appears correctly. It is best to catch problems ahead of time instead of relying on complaints from your visitors.
  • Check your websites on a regular basis for typos, broken links, and images that do not load correctly.
  • Whether it's HTML or PHP, Code on your Own.
